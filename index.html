Meet Our Chancellor

David Bowie

The Starman himself, David Bowie, serves as the honorary chancellor of Meme School. With his chameleon-like ability to adapt and innovate, Bowie embodies the spirit of meme culture.

Under his guidance, our institution pushes the boundaries of creativity and self-expression, just as Bowie did throughout his legendary career.
